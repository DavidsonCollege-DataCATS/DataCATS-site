# About Data CATS

Data CATS (Consulting, Analytics, and Tutoring Services) is a peer-consulting program at Davidson College that connects students, faculty, and staff with trained student consultants for help on data-driven coursework, research, and projects.

## What we can help you with

Our consultants come from a range of majors and bring a mix of general skill areas like statistics, data visualization, database programming, and machine learning, as well as experience in specific languages and tools like Python, R, SQL, and Power BI. Whether you're debugging a script, designing a study, cleaning a messy dataset, or just not sure where to start, a Data CATS consultant can help.

## Where to find us

The Data CATS lab is in **Chambers 3146**. 

- **Browse consultants** on the [homepage](/DataCATS-site/) and filter by skill area, tool, major, coursework, or language to find the best match for your project.
- **Drop in** during office hours, no appointment needed! See the homepage for the current location and schedule.
- **Book an appointment** with a consultant. Browse their profiles and find an open slot on their calendar, or check the [team calendar](/DataCATS-site/calendar) to browse everyone's availability at once.
